word2vec

2016-07-14
machine learning
deep learning/machine learning/nlp

Main idea of word2vec

  • Instead of capturing cooccurrence counts directly
  • Predict surrounding words of every word
  • Faster and can easily incorporate a new sentence/document or add a word to the vocabulary

Details

  • Predict surrounding words in a window of length \(c\) of every word.
  • Objective function: Maximize the \(log\) probability of any context word given the current center word: \[ J(\theta)=\frac{1}{T} \sum_{t=1}^T \sum_{-c\leq j \leq c,j \neq 0} log p(w_{t+j}|w_t) \] \[ p(w_{t+j}|w_t)=p(w_o|w_i)=\frac{exp(v_{w_o}'^T v_{w_i})}{\sum_{w=1}^W exp(v_w'^T v_{w_i})} \] where \(v\) and \(v'\) are input and output vector representations of \(w\) (so every word has two vectors)
